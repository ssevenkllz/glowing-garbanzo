# ZERO CORP — TRAVEL & EXPENSE POLICY HANDBOOK
## Section 2: Booking Policy

### 2.1 Airfare — Class of Service Rules
Zero Corp's airfare policy is designed to balance traveler comfort with cost responsibility.

**Domestic Flights (U.S. only):**
- Economy/Coach class is required for all flights under 5 hours
- Premium Economy is permitted for flights 5+ hours with manager approval
- Business class is not authorized for domestic travel under any standard circumstance

**International Flights:**
- Economy/Coach is required for flights under 6 hours
- Premium Economy is permitted for flights 6–9 hours (no additional approval required)
- Business class is permitted for flights 9+ hours with VP approval
- First class is never authorized

**Note on Upgrades:** Personal upgrades using miles or points are permitted at no cost to Zero Corp. Zero Corp will not reimburse upgrade fees.

### 2.2 Refundable vs. Non-Refundable Fare Policy
Zero Corp's default position is to book the **lowest logical fare** available at time of booking.

- **Non-refundable fares** are acceptable when the trip is confirmed and unlikely to change
- **Refundable fares** may be booked when the trip has a reasonable probability of cancellation or date change (e.g., contract-dependent travel, client-driven scheduling). A brief justification note must be entered in Concur at time of booking.
- **Same-day change fees** are reimbursable when the change is business-driven and documented
- **Personal change fees** (changing travel dates for personal reasons) are not reimbursable

If a non-refundable ticket is cancelled for business reasons, the unused ticket credit must be tracked in Concur and applied to the next Zero Corp booking before expiration.

### 2.3 Hotel — Nightly Rate Limits by City/Region
Zero Corp sets nightly hotel rate caps (excluding taxes and fees) based on destination market:

| Market Tier | Example Cities | Nightly Cap |
|---|---|---|
| Tier 1 — Major Metro | NYC, SF, Boston, DC, Chicago, LA | $350/night |
| Tier 2 — Secondary Metro | Austin, Denver, Seattle, Miami | $250/night |
| Tier 3 — Standard Markets | Kansas City, Lawrence, Columbus | $175/night |
| Tier 4 — International | Varies by destination | GSA OCONUS rates apply |

Rates above the cap require manager approval and documented justification (e.g., conference hotel block, no alternatives available). International per diem rates follow the U.S. Department of State Foreign Per Diem Rates (aoprals.state.gov).

Hotel stays must be for the minimum nights required for the business purpose. Extending a stay for personal travel is permitted but the personal nights are the employee's expense.

### 2.4 Ground Transportation
**Rental Cars:**
- Authorized when public transit or rideshare is not practical
- Mid-size or smaller vehicle required unless group travel warrants larger
- Loss Damage Waiver (LDW) coverage: **decline** — Zero Corp's corporate Amex provides this coverage
- Personal use of rental vehicles is not covered under Zero Corp's policy

**Rideshare (Uber/Lyft):**
- Preferred for airport transfers and local transport in metro areas
- Must be expensed through Zero Corp's linked business account in Concur
- Reasonable tips are reimbursable (up to 20%)

**Personal Vehicle Mileage:**
- Reimbursed at the current IRS standard mileage rate (check current rate at irs.gov/tax-professionals/standard-mileage-rates)
- Mileage must be logged in Concur with origin, destination, and business purpose
- Tolls and parking are reimbursable with receipt

**Rail:**
- Amtrak and regional rail are encouraged where practical
- Business class rail is permitted for trips over 3 hours

### 2.5 Booking Outside Preferred Vendors
Booking outside Zero Corp's preferred vendor network requires a documented exception submitted through Concur prior to booking. Acceptable reasons include:

- Preferred vendor not available for the required dates/route
- Preferred vendor rate exceeds policy cap with no alternatives
- Medical or accessibility requirement not met by preferred vendor

Exceptions are reviewed by the Travel Desk within 1 business day. Booking outside preferred vendors without an approved exception may result in partial or denied reimbursement.

### 2.6 Booking Tool Requirements
All Zero Corp travel must be booked through **Concur Travel** (concur.zerocorp.com) unless an exception is approved by the Travel Desk.

Booking outside Concur (e.g., directly on airline or hotel websites) is permitted only for emergency same-day travel and must be submitted for reimbursement within 5 business days with full documentation.

Contact the Travel Desk at travel@zerocorp.com or (800) 555-0192 for booking assistance.
