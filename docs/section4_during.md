# ZERO CORP — TRAVEL & EXPENSE POLICY HANDBOOK
## Section 4: During Travel

### 4.1 What's Covered vs. Not Covered On the Road
**COVERED (reimbursable with receipt):**
- Airfare, rail, and ground transportation (within policy)
- Hotel (within nightly rate limits)
- Meals (within per diem limits — see 4.2)
- Business calls and essential roaming charges
- Laundry for trips exceeding 5 consecutive nights
- Checked baggage (1 bag domestic, 2 bags international)
- Tips on covered services (up to 20%)
- Wi-Fi (on flights and at hotels when not included)
- Parking at airport or client site (with receipt)

**NOT COVERED (non-reimbursable):**
- Alcohol (except at approved client entertainment — see entertainment policy)
- In-room movies or minibar
- Personal travel extensions
- Traffic or parking violations
- Lost or stolen personal items
- Travel insurance purchased separately (covered under corporate Amex)
- Gym fees and spa services
- Souvenirs or personal shopping
- Companion/spouse travel

### 4.2 Meals & Per Diem — Daily Limits by City
Zero Corp uses a tiered per diem system based on destination. Per diem covers breakfast, lunch, and dinner combined. Receipts are required for individual meal expenses over $25.

| Market Tier | Example Cities | Daily Meal Per Diem |
|---|---|---|
| Tier 1 — Major Metro | NYC, SF, DC, Chicago | $100/day |
| Tier 2 — Secondary Metro | Austin, Denver, Seattle | $75/day |
| Tier 3 — Standard Markets | Kansas City, Columbus | $60/day |
| International | Varies | U.S. State Dept. M&IE rates apply |

Current U.S. State Department per diem rates for international destinations are available at aoprals.state.gov.

**First and last day of travel:** Per diem is prorated at 75% of the applicable daily rate.

**Team meals:** When a Zero Corp employee pays for a team meal, the full amount is reimbursable at the Tier 1 rate per person, with manager approval and a list of attendees and business purpose in Concur.

### 4.3 Incidentals Policy
Incidentals (tips, small sundry purchases, etc.) are included in the per diem and are not separately reimbursed. The exception is tips on covered transportation (Uber, taxi, shuttle) which are reimbursable up to 20% separately.

Hotel incidental holds placed by the hotel are the traveler's responsibility to resolve at checkout.

### 4.4 Flight Delays and Cancellations — Traveler Rights
When your flight is delayed or cancelled:

**Delay under 2 hours:**
- No action required beyond your original itinerary
- Meals during delay are reimbursable up to $25 with receipt

**Delay 2–4 hours:**
- Contact the airline directly for rebooking options
- Meals reimbursable up to $50 with receipt
- No hotel required unless overnight becomes necessary

**Delay over 4 hours or overnight:**
- Contact Zero Corp Travel Desk immediately: (800) 555-0192
- Hotel (up to Tier 1 rate) and meals are fully reimbursable
- The Travel Desk will assist with rebooking

**Flight Cancellation:**
- Contact airline first for rebooking on next available flight
- If airline cannot rebook within 4 hours, contact Travel Desk
- Corporate Amex travel insurance provides trip cancellation coverage — call the number on the back of your card for claims

**Know your rights:** Under DOT regulations, airlines must offer refunds for cancelled flights regardless of fare type. The Travel Desk can advise on specific airline policies.

### 4.5 Rebooking — Who Can Authorize and How
**Standard rebooking** (same airline, next available flight): No additional approval needed — handle directly with the airline or through Concur mobile.

**Change of airline or significant itinerary change**: Contact Travel Desk at (800) 555-0192. Travel Desk has authority to authorize rebooking up to $500 above original fare without manager approval.

**Changes over $500 above original fare**: Travel Desk will contact your manager for approval in real time.

Always retain all receipts and boarding passes for rebooking claims.

### 4.6 Lost Luggage and Travel Emergencies
**Lost or delayed luggage:**
- File a claim with the airline immediately at baggage claim
- Essential replacement items (clothing, toiletries) are reimbursable up to $150/day while luggage is delayed, with receipts
- Contact Travel Desk if luggage is not recovered within 24 hours

**Medical emergency:**
- Call 911 or local emergency services immediately
- Notify your manager as soon as safely possible
- Contact Zero Corp's 24/7 emergency line: (800) 555-0911
- Zero Corp's corporate Amex provides emergency medical assistance — call the number on the back of your card
- Medical expenses incurred during travel are reimbursable with documentation

**Security incident or personal safety concern:**
- Remove yourself from the situation first
- Contact local authorities if needed
- Contact Zero Corp Security: security@zerocorp.com / (800) 555-0199 (24/7)
- Do not attempt to resolve security incidents alone
