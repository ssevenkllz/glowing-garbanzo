# ZERO CORP — TRAVEL & EXPENSE POLICY HANDBOOK
## Section 6: Post-Trip & Reimbursement

### 6.1 Expense Report Submission Deadlines
Zero Corp requires expense reports to be submitted within **10 business days** of trip completion. Late submissions are subject to the following:

- **11–20 business days late**: Manager notification required; Finance flags for review
- **21–30 business days late**: VP approval required to process
- **Over 30 business days**: Expense may be denied at Finance discretion; employee is responsible for the expense

**Year-end deadline**: All expenses incurred in a calendar year must be submitted by December 20th of that year. No exceptions.

Submit all expense reports through Concur (concur.zerocorp.com) under "Expense" → "Create New Report."

### 6.2 Required Receipts and Documentation
Zero Corp requires original receipts (digital or physical) for all expenses over $25. For expenses under $25, receipts are strongly encouraged but not mandatory.

**Required for all submissions:**
- Business purpose for the trip (1–2 sentences)
- List of attendees for any meal or entertainment over $50
- Receipts for all individual items over $25

**Required for specific expense types:**
- **Hotel**: Itemized hotel folio (not just the credit card slip)
- **Airfare**: E-ticket confirmation or booking receipt
- **Car rental**: Rental agreement and final receipt
- **Mileage**: Log with origin, destination, date, and miles
- **International expenses**: Currency conversion documentation (Concur handles this automatically if booked through the platform)

Missing receipts can be documented using the Concur "Missing Receipt Affidavit" for items under $75. This should be used sparingly.

### 6.3 Approved Expense Categories
When submitting in Concur, use the following expense categories:

| Category | Examples |
|---|---|
| Airfare | Flights, seat upgrades (if policy-compliant) |
| Lodging | Hotel, extended stay |
| Ground Transportation | Taxi, rideshare, rental car, parking, tolls |
| Meals — Business | Meals while traveling (within per diem) |
| Meals — Team/Client | Group meals with attendee list |
| Mileage | Personal vehicle at IRS rate |
| Communications | Business calls, roaming, Wi-Fi |
| Baggage | Checked bags within policy |
| Conference/Registration | Conference fees (pre-approved) |
| Incidentals | Tips on transportation (up to 20%) |
| Emergency Expenses | Medical, security-related, documented exceptions |

Do not use generic categories like "Other" without a written explanation in the notes field.

### 6.4 Reimbursement Timelines
Once an expense report is submitted and fully approved in Concur:

- **Standard reimbursement**: Processed within **5 business days** of final approval
- **Payment method**: Direct deposit to employee's registered bank account
- **Corporate Amex charges**: Automatically reconciled; employee does not receive separate reimbursement for Amex purchases (Zero Corp pays the Amex bill directly)

If reimbursement has not appeared within 7 business days of final approval, contact Finance at expenses@zerocorp.com.

### 6.5 Common Rejection Reasons and How to Avoid Them
The following are the most common reasons expense reports are flagged or rejected:

| Rejection Reason | How to Avoid |
|---|---|
| Missing receipt over $25 | Attach all receipts at time of submission |
| No business purpose noted | Always include 1–2 sentence description |
| Expense exceeds policy limit | Note exception reason or get pre-approval |
| Alcohol included without approval | Separate alcohol from meals; get entertainment approval |
| Submitted after deadline | Submit within 10 business days of return |
| Hotel folio not itemized | Request itemized folio at checkout |
| Missing attendee list for group meal | List all attendees and their company/role |
| Personal expenses mixed in | Separate personal charges before submitting |

If an expense is rejected, you will receive a notification in Concur with the specific reason. You have **5 business days** to correct and resubmit before the report is closed.

### 6.6 Feedback and Trip Close-Out Requirements
Zero Corp uses trip feedback to improve travel policy, vendor relationships, and the Copilot experience.

**Required for all trips:**
- Confirm trip completion in Concur within 2 business days of return
- Submit expense report within 10 business days

**Requested (not required):**
- Complete the 2-minute trip feedback survey sent via email post-trip
- Flag any vendor issues (hotel, airline, car rental) in the Travel Desk portal

**Automatic close-out items handled by the Copilot:**
- Approval records are marked complete
- Open hotel/flight bookings are reconciled
- Reminder sent if expense report has not been started within 5 business days of return

If your trip was cancelled after booking, confirm the cancellation in Concur and work with the Travel Desk to recover any unused credits or obtain refunds.
